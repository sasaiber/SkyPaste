# Privacy Policy - SkyPaste

**Last Updated: March 16, 2026**

## Overview

SkyPaste is committed to protecting your privacy. This is a **local-first application** that respects your data and operates entirely on your machine. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

---

## Key Privacy Principles

### 🔒 All Data Stays Local

- **Zero Server Communication**: SkyPaste does **not** send any clipboard data, user information, or metadata to remote servers
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your clipboard history, snippets, and folders are stored exclusively on your machine
- **No Tracking**: We do not use analytics, telemetry, or tracking libraries
- **No Advertisements**: SkyPaste is completely ad-free and does not contain any advertising software

### 📱 What Data is Processed

SkyPaste handles the following data **entirely on your device**:

- **Clipboard content**: Text and images you copy
- **Clipboard history**: Previous clipboard items for recall
- **Notes and snippets**: Any custom text you save
- **Folder organization**: Your custom folder structure with emojis and colors
- **Settings**: Your preferences and hotkey configurations
- **Search history**: Temporary search queries (not stored between sessions unless you explicitly save them)

### 💾 How Data is Stored

- All data is stored in the local file system of your macOS machine
- Your clipboard history is saved in local databases that only you can access
- File access requires your macOS system login
- No encryption is enforced by default (you can use macOS full-disk encryption for additional protection)

### 🚫 What We Don't Do

- ❌ Upload clipboard content to any server
- ❌ Transmit your data to the cloud
- ❌ Share your data with third parties
- ❌ Track your app usage or behavior
- ❌ Collect personal information
- ❌ Use cookies or tracking pixels
- ❌ Access your data without your explicit action

### 🔐 Security Practices

- **Application Sandboxing**: Runs with the security constraints of macOS
- **Accessibility Permissions**: Requires accessibility access for auto-paste functionality (a standard macOS permission)
- **No External Dependencies**: Minimized dependencies reduce potential security risks
- **Open Source Friendly**: Code is designed to be transparent and auditable

### 🎯 Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

- **View**: All your data is visible through the SkyPaste interface
- **Export**: You can export your clipboard history at any time
- **Delete**: You can delete individual items or clear all history
- **Uninstall**: Simply remove the app - all associated data can be manually deleted from your file system

---

## Accessibility & Permissions

SkyPaste requires **Accessibility permissions** to enable:

- Global hotkey detection
- Auto-paste functionality
- Clipboard monitoring

These permissions are required to operate the app's core features and are used **only for these purposes**. We do not use accessibility permissions to monitor or record your activities outside of the SkyPaste app.

---

## macOS System Access

SkyPaste accesses standard macOS system features:

- **Clipboard Access**: To read and write clipboard content
- **File System Access**: To store your clipboard history in local databases
- **Keyboard Input**: To detect global hotkeys

All access is **local to your device** and does not involve remote communication.

---

## Updates & Support

When you manually check for updates through the Settings menu, the app may connect to a remote server to check the latest version availability. This check:

- Only verifies if a newer version exists
- Does not transmit your clipboard data or personal information
- Only contains the request for version information
- Can be disabled in the Settings

---

## Third-Party Components

SkyPaste is built on the Maccy clipboard manager architecture and uses only system frameworks from macOS. Any third-party code used is carefully selected and does not include telemetry or tracking.

---

## Changes to This Policy

SkyPaste respects your privacy and will continue to do so. If this policy changes in future versions, we will clearly communicate those changes through the app release notes.

---

## Contact & Questions

For questions about this privacy policy or SkyPaste's privacy practices:

- **Review the source code**: The application's architecture is designed to be transparent
- **Check the settings**: All features and permissions are visible in the Settings menu
- **Examine local storage**: Your data files are stored in standard macOS locations

---

## Compliance

SkyPaste is designed to comply with privacy regulations including:

- **GDPR** (General Data Protection Regulation)
- **CCPA** (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **PIPEDA** (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)

Since SkyPaste does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on remote servers, these regulations naturally apply to your local data management.

---

## Summary

**SkyPaste keeps your clipboard private. Everything stays on your machine. Nothing is sent to our servers. You have complete control over your data.**

Enjoy using SkyPaste knowing that your clipboard history is yours and yours alone!
